We provide full-service legal representation to companies and individuals for all of their legal needs, large and small.

Our partners have extensive experience in civil and commercial law, acquired at leading companies and law firms in Israel, the United States and Asia.

Corporate & Commercial

  • Commercial agreements
  • Corporate structuring and governance
  • Bankruptcy and liquidation
  • Intellectual property
  • Energy law
  • ​Negotiation and mediation

International Business

  • International commercial transactions
  • International subsidiaries, joint ventures, mergers and acquisitions
  • International taxation
  • Translation of legal documents

Private Individuals

  • Wills and estates
  • Real property purchases and sales
  • Trusts and taxation
  • Visa and immigration applications
  • International adoption
