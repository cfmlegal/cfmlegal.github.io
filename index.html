Partners

Jeff Cymet jeff@cfmlegal.com

Jeff has been advising and representing a wide range of business and individual clients in Israel and internationally for over twenty years. His expertise is in helping businesses and individuals navigate the requirements of doing business in Israel, and in structuring and negotiating corporate and commercial transactions internationally. Jeff has worked at the leading law firms in Israel, New York and South Korea, in-house at one of Israel's leading manufacturing companies, and as legal adviser to the Israeli Ministry of Justice.

Alexander Fedan alex@cfmlegal.com

Alexander specializes in commercial and corporate law and international taxation, and is the head of our China business practice. He also engages in corporate governance, labor law, energy and environmental matters and international adoption cases.

Alan Meerkin alan@cfmlegal.com

Alan has significant legal experience in both private practice and in public organizations, including the Hebrew University and the Movement for Quality Government. He served as co-director of the office of the Official Receiver specializing in bankruptcy and corporate liquidation, and represented the Administrator General and the State Attorney General. In 2000 Alan established a boutique translation office providing high quality translations to lawyers and professionals in other fields.
